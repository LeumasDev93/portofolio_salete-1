BUDDY
Our app, BUDDY, the Virtual Friend, transforms reading into a social and enjoyable experience. It accompanies you on any page if you wish, offering comments, explanations, and answers to questions. If you pause your reading, it sends encouraging messages with spoilers or even just checks in on you. The goal is to enrich the reading experience, making it interactive and personalized, promoting the habit of reading, and turning this activity into a more communicative one.
Objective
To encourage reading with reminders and support for all readers, promoting the habit of reading. Provide an interactive and enjoyable experience with “Buddy,” who makes comments and funny observations about the story and characters. It also offers personalized recommendations for new books based on the user’s preferences and reading history.

Emotion
During the creation of this project, we encountered some challenges, such as obtaining accurate and relevant data for the journey map. This requires a deep understanding of customer behavior to make it as comprehensive as possible. Additionally, designing a user-friendly and intuitive interface was essential to appeal to both avid and beginner readers or people unaccustomed to digital platforms. The app must be accessible and easy to use, avoiding overwhelming users with excessive information or features.
