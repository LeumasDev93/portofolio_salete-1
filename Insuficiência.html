Insuficiência cardíaca
This pamphlet was created to inform and raise awareness about heart failure, addressing its causes, symptoms, and treatments. Our goal is to facilitate understanding and promote preventive care to improve quality of life.
Objective
To explore the relationship between Design and Health and promote the practice of Information Design applied to this field. To apply the knowledge acquired to the redesign (informational brochures) and the design of new communication materials for use in the various units of the Lisbon Western SNS (National Health Service).
Target Audience:
Patients of the National Health Service (SNS) with heart failure and their families.
Format:
Designed to be reproducible on printers or photocopiers in health centers and hospitals.
Accessibility:
Intended for a diverse audience, ensuring clarity and usefulness for different profiles.
