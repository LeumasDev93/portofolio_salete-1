Stones Against Diamonds
Humanity Against the Tide is a zine that explores how the ephemeral, movement in time, pollution as a result of human behavior, technologies, and the melancholy of the masses shape contemporary society.
Objective
To criticize the alienation of the human being in relation to the changes in the world around them.
